About

Commune is a community tourism program that allows you to broaden your travel routes and help to make a difference. Meet local rural and indigenous families first hand, take part of their traditions, try local comfort food and truly walk the road less traveled. With each activity, to help to bring sustainable economical development to communities off the beaten path.

Here is how works

We map communities in underdeveloped rural areas close to traditional tourist destinations and give them training and counseling to host guests and offer touristic services. Once that is done, we upload them in our online booking platform by creating a complete profile. Through the website, you can then filter and search for the accommodations that best suit your needs, and book your stay. When you stay with a community, you can also book alternative tours, order local comfort food and participate in to supporting the community's structural and economical development.

It is that simple! You travel, you transform.
