About

Meet local indigenous and rural families first hand, take part in their traditions, try local food, explore alternative tours and truly walk a road less traveled.

How it works

Travel with a cause

Your money will help indigenous rural communities to improve their surroundings while you have the time of your life getting to know their traditions and culture.

Customize your travel

The communities offer a vast variety of activities and experiences that you can choose from depending on your interests. Choose the type of stay, food and workshop.

Request a book

You can book online your stay with us and start experiencing and giving your travel a social meaning/purpose. We charge a small operational fee to facilitate this process and to continue searching for amazing communities to explore.

It is that simple! You travel, you transform.
